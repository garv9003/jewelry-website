Our Story

Generations of passion for the world's finest gemstones, born in the gem city of Jaipur.

A Legacy of Excellence

Crafted with passion since 1996

At Durgesh Jewels, our journey began in 1996 with a dream, strong determination, and a passion for the world of gemstones. Founded by my father from humble beginnings, the company was built on the values of honesty, hard work, trust, and exceptional quality. With years of dedication and deep knowledge of gemstones, he earned the confidence of every customer by offering authenticity and excellence in every piece. What started as a small vision gradually became a trusted name, carrying forward a legacy of craftsmanship, tradition, and commitment. Today, Durgesh Jewels proudly continues that legacy by blending timeless traditions with modern elegance. Every gemstone reflects our passion, heritage, and dedication to delivering beauty, quality, and trust to our customers. For us, jewellery is not just an ornament — it is a symbol of emotions, culture, and timeless memories passed from one generation to another.

What Sets Us Apart
